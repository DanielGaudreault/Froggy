## How to Play

- Use **arrow keys** (‚Üë ‚Üì ‚Üê ‚Üí) to move the frog
- Avoid the **cars** on the road
- Jump on **logs** to cross the river
- Reach the **green safe zones** to score points
- You have **3 lives** - don't lose them all!
